About Our School

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

GS Bubazi is a distinguished secondary school nestled in the heart of Rubengera, Karongi District, Western Rwanda. Our institution stands as a beacon of quality education, nurturing brilliant minds through a rigorous curriculum and a values-driven environment.

We believe every student carries extraordinary potential. Our dedicated staff, modern teaching methods, and strong community ties create the ideal conditions for students to discover, grow, and excel.

🎯
Our Mission
To provide quality, inclusive education that empowers every student to achieve their highest potential.
🔭
Our Vision
To be the leading secondary school in Karongi, recognized for academic distinction and character development.
⚖️
Integrity
We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all we do.
🤝
Community
Strong partnerships with families, local leaders, and Rwanda's broader educational community.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

GS Bubazi offers both Ordinary Level (O-Level) for lower secondary and Advanced Level (A-Level) in the Science stream.

Enrollment is done through the national school placement system or by visiting the school office directly. Bring academic transcripts, birth certificate, and a passport photo.

GS Bubazi is a day school only. All students attend classes during the day and return home in the evening.

GS Bubazi consistently maintains a national exam pass rate above 90%, placing us among the top-performing schools in Karongi District.

We offer Science Club, English Club, and Environment Club, plus an annual Sports Day that brings the whole school community together.

Each teacher's phone and email is visible on their staff card above. You can also contact the school office to be connected with the right person.
